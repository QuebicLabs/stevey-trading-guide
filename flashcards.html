RULES

The 6 Rules That Keep You In The Game

1. The chart enters for you

Gold line = where you get in. You don't guess the entry — the indicator drew it.

2. The red line is law

Red line = your stop. If price hits it, you're out. No "let me give it room." That's how accounts die.

3. Take profit at the green lines

Green lines = your targets, with the dollar amount on each. Take some off at TP1, let the rest run.

4. Orange = wait, Green/Red = go

Orange "WATCH" arrow means get ready, hands off. Only the green/red arrow is permission to click.

5. One setup at a time

One trade, one contract while learning. Win or lose, log it. No revenge clicking after a loss.

6. No killzone, no trade

If the background isn't tinted, close the platform. The edge only exists inside the killzones.
